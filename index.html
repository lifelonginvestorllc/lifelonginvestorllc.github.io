Introduce LIATS 介绍交易系统

LIATS (Lifelong Investor Algorithmic Trading System) is agile and transparent, applies the most suitable trading strategy to well-picked security target (Stock/ETF/Future/Forex/Option) to match its trending pattern. By connecting to your own IB (InteractiveBrokers) account, our Algo Bot will conduct the trading session under your monitor and also notify you through SMS, Telegram or Email as per your preference.
